# Alex Writing Shape

Use this after raw material exists.

## Workflow

1. Identify thesis and reader.
2. Propose candidate openings.
3. Choose structure.
4. Draft or revise one section at a time.
5. Decide where lists, tables, callouts, quotes, or examples help.
6. End with title options and final edit checklist.
