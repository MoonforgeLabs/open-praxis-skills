# Alex Edit Article

Use this for revision, not raw generation.

## Workflow

1. Identify target reader and thesis.
2. Map the current structure.
3. Find weak transitions, repetition, unsupported claims, and unclear stakes.
4. Suggest or apply edits at the right level: outline, section, paragraph, or sentence.
5. Preserve voice unless the user asks for a new style.
